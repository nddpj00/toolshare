Who we are

A community organisation based in Deal, Kent - with many useful tools and items, from ladders to lawnmowers for local people to borrow, with just a small donation.

We have power tools, hand tools, garden tools, ladders and even roof boxes for your car.

Why its good to share

Save yourself money.

Save space at home having to store the item.

Better for the planet - less materials like plastic being produced, which can't then easily be recycled.

Be part of your community

Our Tools

We have an ever-growing list of items you can borrow. Have a browse and plan your next project. Also check out our events and blog for inspiration!

Browse Tools!

How it works

First -

Browse our tools and add as many as you need to your bag.

Second -

Complete your purchase with our online payment system.

Third -

Await your email confirming your order and pop down to the Bear Cave(our shop) to pick up your tools.

Just check the shop opening times here before coming along.

Unsure how to operate the item?
Let our helpful member of staff know when collecting and they'll happily demonstrate.
